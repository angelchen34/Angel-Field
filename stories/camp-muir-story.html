MEMORIAL DAY...Mostly sunny with a slight chance of showers.
Snow level near 7500 feet.

Forecasters use freezing levels when the forecast is dry. But when precipitation is expected, the forecast will change mention to a "snow level" instead. Snow levels are given at a lower altitude than freezing levels simply because snow can fall at temperatures slightly above freezing.
